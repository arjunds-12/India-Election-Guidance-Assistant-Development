Voter Registration Guide

A simple step-by-step checklist to get your Voter ID (EPIC Card).

1

Prepare Documents

Gather your passport-size photo, Address proof (Aadhaar, Passport, or Utility bill), and Age proof.

2

Fill Form 6

Apply online via the Voter Helpline App or the NVSP portal. Form 6 is for new registrations.

Open NVSP Portal
3

Verification (BLO)

A Booth Level Officer (BLO) may visit your address to verify the details you provided.

4

Receive EPIC Card

Your Voter ID will be sent via speed post to your registered address within a few weeks.
